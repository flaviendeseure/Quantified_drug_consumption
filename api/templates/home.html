Drugs consumption quantified

Various studies have outlined that personality attributes are related with drug consumption. The objective of this project is to predict the probability of the consumption of different licit and illicit drugs on the basis of personality.

We use the UCI Drug consumption (quantified) Data Set, you can get more information about this dataset by using the link below.

Go to UCI dataset »

Personnality test used


NEO-FFI-R

The Big Five personality test measures the five personality factors that psychologists have determined are core to our personality makeup.

View details »

BIS11

Barratt Impulsiveness Scale (BIS-11) is a questionnaire designed to assess the personality/behavioral construct of impulsiveness.

View details »

ImpSS

The ImpSS scale is a 19 question true-false scale assessing various personality characteristics and behaviors related to impulsivity and sensation seeking, and it is scored by summing the items that are consistent with impulsivity or sensation seeking.
